About Me


Hello! My name is Will and I am a Self-Taught Photo & Video Editor.

Photography was one of the first hobbies I picked up as a kid. I was driven by how amazing it was to capture moments through a click of a button - I was really into it, I would go out of my way to get the best shot that I could. When I got my first camera in high school it took me up a level. In 2017 of my senior year, I was a finalist of the Photographer's Magazine Forum for high school/college students. In 2018, I shot entertainment, product, landscape, travel, and enterprise photography. Then in 2019, my passion for automotive photography grew - I began to be well known in the car community. Finally 2020, I began my videography journey - I shot music videos, real estate, street, and automotive.

Since then, photo and video has taken me everywhere. It has been a key to opening many doors - from meeting amazing people, to traveling, and experiencing life. I aspire to keep this a life-long hobby and to grow with it.
